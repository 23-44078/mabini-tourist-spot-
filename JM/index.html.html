Experience Mabini

World-Class Diving

Anilao in Mabini is renowned as one of the Philippines' premier diving destinations. With over 40 dive sites featuring vibrant coral reefs, diverse marine life, and excellent visibility, it's a paradise for both beginner and experienced divers.

Anilao Beach

Discover secluded white sand beaches with crystal-clear waters perfect for swimming, snorkeling, and relaxation. Our beaches offer the perfect escape from the hustle and bustle of city life.

Mountain Adventures

Mt. Gulugod Baboy offers one of the most scenic hiking experiences in Batangas. The relatively easy trail rewards hikers with breathtaking 360-degree views of Batangas Bay, Taal Lake, and the surrounding countryside.

Authentic Food

Savor the rich flavors of Batangueño cuisine with fresh seafood, traditional Filipino dishes, and local specialties. From grilled fish to authentic adobo, experience the true taste of the Philippines.
